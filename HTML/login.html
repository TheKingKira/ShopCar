A DIVISION OF THE JOHN SCOTTI AUTOMOTIVE GROUP

Welcome to your Lamborghini dealer in Montreal, a proud member of the John Scotti Automotive Group since 1979. As the very first Lamborghini franchise in Canada and in North America, we have earned the trust of a demanding clientele that seeks world-class performance and exclusive Italian quality. Among our wide selection of new Lamborghinis for sale, these prestigious cars that are as innovative as they are spectacular speak for themselves: Lamborghini Aventador S, Lamborghini Huracán EVO and Lamborghini Urus, a stunning SUV incorporating the DNA of a super sports car.

PRE-OWNED LAMBORGHINI INVENTORY

Lamborghini Montreal occupies a special place in the pre-owned universe of luxury cars in Canada and North America. Whether you’re a first time buyer or an experienced collector, you’ll find a large inventory of pre-owned Lamborghinis available at our Kirkland dealership, recent or classic, in their premium genuine condition. Our pre-owned Lamborghini department displays very low mileage vehicles that were maintained with exceptional care.

AFTER-SALES SERVICE

As a true work of art on the road, every Lamborghini is hand assembled in Italy by highly qualified specialists. That is why, at Lamborghini Montreal, we pay careful attention to each of our customers, by offering a complete range of Lamborghini services including service maintenance, mechanical repairs, body shop as well as customization of new and pre-owned vehicles.
